About Samreen Real Estate & Builders

With over 25 years in Thiruvannamalai’s real estate market, Samreen Real Estate & Builders has helped hundreds of families and investors secure prime plots and build dream homes. Our unmatched local expertise covers both land and residential buildings—guided by trust, transparency, and an eye for long-term value.

Our Story

Samreen Real Estate & Builders began its journey over 25 years ago, with a clear mission: making property buying simple, safe, and rewarding for everyone in Thiruvannamalai. Today, we combine deep-rooted local knowledge with modern construction techniques to deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower clients to make informed real estate investments and create quality living spaces in Thiruvannamalai.

Vision: To be the leading choice for property and building solutions, recognised for our reliability, customer care, and commitment to excellence.
